A Bit About Me

Being born to Brazilian diplomats, my childhood consisted of moving to a new country about every three years. I finally settled in NYC for college at Fordham University, where I completed a B.A. in Mathematics and a minor in Visual Arts.

Web development has helped me bridge the gap between Mathematics and Visual Arts.

I'm currently working as a Software Engineer at a startup (Appallicious) based in San Francisco. Although I have been working as a full-stack developer, front-end development is what I'm most excited about. I love transforming beautiful designs into user friendly applications and websites.

In my free time I enjoy rock climbing and hiking around the Bay Area.
